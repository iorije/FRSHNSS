FRESHNESS is an association by the HipHop Community for the HipHop Community. Our main goal is to create a platform where people can meet, learn from and have fun with eachother in an HipHop environment. We mainly organize training sessions which are open and free for everyone. These are not lessons but we do use the "each one, teach one" principle.
